Why Choose Our Layer Feeds? Kwa Nini Uchague Chakula Chetu cha Kuku wa Mayai?

Maximum Egg Production Uzalishaji wa Juu wa Mayai

Achieve up to 95% peak production rate with our scientifically balanced nutrition program. Fikia hadi 95% kiwango cha uzalishaji wa kilele kwa mpango wetu wa lishe uliosawazishwa kwa kisayansi.

Strong Shell Quality Ubora wa Maganda Imara

High calcium and phosphorus levels ensure strong, crack-resistant eggshells for better marketability. Viwango vya juu vya kalsiamu na fosforasi vinahakikisha maganda imara, yasiyo na uwezo wa kupasuka kwa uuzaji bora.

Extended Production Uzalishaji Ulioongezwa

Maintain consistent laying performance for up to 72 weeks with optimal nutrition support. Dumisha utendaji thabiti wa kutaga kwa wiki hadi 72 kwa msaada wa lishe bora.

Cost Efficiency Ufanisi wa Gharama

Excellent feed conversion ratio of 2.1:1 maximizes egg production per kilogram of feed consumed. Uwiano bora wa ubadilishaji wa chakula wa 2.1:1 unaongeza uzalishaji wa mayai kwa kilo ya chakula kilichotumika.

Bird Health Afya ya Ndege

Enhanced immune system support reduces mortality and maintains flock health throughout production. Msaada ulioboreshwa wa mfumo wa kinga unapunguza vifo na kudumisha afya ya kundi katika uzalishaji wote.

Scientific Formulation Mchanganyiko wa Kisayansi

Developed by poultry nutritionists using the latest research in layer nutrition and egg production. Imeundwa na wataalamu wa lishe ya kuku wakitumia utafiti wa hivi karibuni katika lishe ya kuku wa mayai na uzalishaji wa mayai.
