Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?
We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM East Africa Time. We're closed on weekends and public holidays, but you can still send us messages through our contact form.
How quickly do you respond to inquiries?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters received via phone are typically addressed immediately during business hours.
What information should I include in my inquiry?
Please include your contact information, the nature of your inquiry, any specific products or services you're interested in, and any relevant details that will help us provide you with the most accurate information.
Do you offer site visits or consultations?
Yes, we offer on-site consultations for qualified prospects. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll arrange a suitable time for a visit or consultation.
Can I request a quote through this form?
Absolutely! Select "Pricing & Quotes" as your inquiry type and provide as much detail as possible about your requirements. We'll prepare a customized quote for you.
